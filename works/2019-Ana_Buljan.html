Ana Buljan

ALEPH ZETA: The Star 2019

This film is a part of an experiment in constructing a visual language that can be seen as a consequence to her attraction to chance and the unintended.

Ana Buljan [HR] is an artist who works with still and moving images that seek to preserve ephemeral landscapes. Her work pursues the transient, situated through frames of memory and dislocation. Her current research has focused on situating traditional filmmaking methods within contemporary narratives. She also works as a production designer in film and theatre.
