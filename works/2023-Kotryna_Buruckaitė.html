Kotryna Buruckaitė

If I was a Rocket 2023

If I was a Rocket is a multi-linear film essay. Intersecting narratives investigate personal memory, dreams and geological time. A hypnotic and intimate journey reveals the inner world of the narrator.

Kotryna Buruckaitė (b. 1992) - is a Lithuanian visual artist/filmmaker currently based in Amsterdam. In her work she investigates humans in (a)typical settings. Fascinated by scopophilia, the pleasure of observing others, Kotryna depicts real and fictitious characters and their quotidian relationships. With a touch of fiction and absurdity she questions narrative methods and the irregular patterns of mind.
