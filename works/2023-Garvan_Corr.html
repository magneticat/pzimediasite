Garvan Corr

Eir-PG 2023

Eir-PG invites you to peruse the visual machinations of an alternative Ireland . Indulge yourself in this mischievous one-man-show. Through an array of characters and doses of satire, Eir-PG questions the lore of nationhood and generational memory. Garvan interrogates and reincarnates tales of Irish Faux-Lore.

Garvan is a Rotterdam based artist from Dublin and his practice engages in photography, video, drawing, sound and performance. Themes of absurdity, satire and irony arise within his work alongside a healthy dose of playfulness and curiosity.
