Sacha Dufils

Images without Worlds 2022

The photographs in this series depict a world that’s not there and thus become images without worlds. In the context of the Anthropocene, the human epoch, the mountain becomes a monument and the lens has the ability to preserve it. What was previously grand, godlike or sublime, can now only ever be an image.

Sacha Dufils’ work focuses on the relationship between humans and the landscapes around them. Themes as romanticism, escapism and the sublime are at the core of making her work. Sacha’s work is not only about the journey of landscape but also about the journey of photography. She researches both analogue and modern digital techniques within this medium.
