Station de Stroming 2022

Station de Stroming is a nomadic web radio station, conceived and presently based in Rotterdam. This station functions as a journalistic tool to design a series of conversations about and encounters in its surrounding, interrogating and revealing its current socio-political, legal configuration, as well as the ways in which this can be re-imagined.

slvi.e is a researcher, writer and radio maker, interested in community based approaches and non-linear studies of history.

Station de Stroming

The Dutch stroming translates to ‘the current’ and ‘ideological movement’. A tension between water and time. Rotterdam, the Netherlands, formed by and from water, moves in and out of focus. This radio show, like water, which holds and divides us, is expanding itself beyond national borders in the attempt to touch upon broader geo-political tides and imaginaries. Equally shifting through language. You can tune in live at stationdestroming.com as well as explore the archive of the first radio season presented and created in the context of the graduation show.
