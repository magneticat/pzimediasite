All about Fresh to Decay 2017

Fresh to Decay refers to a series of short experimental films about the lives of juveniles, their thoughts, and emotions. The work involves marginalised subjects such as: sex, campus violence, and depression. It comprises of video clips from different platforms along with original dialogue written by the filmmaker. Each short film can be viewed independently as an individual project, or seen as chapters of a larger whole: "Fresh to Decay".
