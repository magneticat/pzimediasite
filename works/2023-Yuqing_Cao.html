Yuqing Cao

One-Bedroom-One 2023

A young lady tried to find strangers to chat on a dating app during her breakup, in order to relieve her loneliness. She stumbled upon a stranger, who locked himself out of the flat, and was forced to waste the time in a nearby park. They all entered this cyberspace to escape the emotional predicament of the moment.

Yuqing Cao (CN), work with images, videos and texts. She pays attention to the intimate relationship between people, constantly excavating the hidden reality in daily life, focusing on the narrative of individual experience, and using images to explore the implicit connection between social space and private emotions.
