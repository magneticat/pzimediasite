Adèle Grégoire

Of all places, the best nights were found in the garden of old ladies 2022

Of all places, the best nights were found in the garden of old ladies is a story where many encounters occur. This time, a community of nomadic ensenia foetidia worms, also called red worms, stumble upon a couple of old ladies and a drawing. Adèle Grégoire expose you to the moments they shared together through an audiovisual installation made of drawings, sound-surrounded by found, reclaimed, recycled, or composted material.

Adèle works with drawing, writing, sound, digital images, composting and garbage.
